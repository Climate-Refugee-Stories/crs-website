Christine Wheatley

Christine Wheatley, PhD, is a scholar of global migration, the Executive Director of New-Age Environmental Development of Africa (NED Africa), and a lecturer and consultant with the International Organization for Migration. For her doctoral work in Sociology, Christine conducted fieldwork with displaced migrants in the United States and Mexico. Her organization now works with communities affected by deforestation and coastal erosion in West Africa to build sustainable new economies.

Location: Hohoe, Ghana
